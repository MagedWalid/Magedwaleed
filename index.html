About Me

Turning Vision Into Unforgettable Live Experiences

I'm Maged Waleed — an Events Manager with 9+ years building and executing live experiences that leave a mark. From Egypt's first IRONMAN 70.3 to multi-day international government summits, I bring both operational precision and strategic instinct to every project.
